# [L4D & 2] Fix Tank Hit Through Wall

### Introduction
- Extra collision tests to prevent tank fist's hit from registering through thin walls.

<hr>

### Requirement
- **DHooks**

<hr>

### Installation
1. Put **plugins/l4d_fix_tank_hit_through_wall.smx** to your _plugins_ folder.
2. Put **gamedata/l4d_fix_tank_hit_through_wall.txt** to your _gamedata_ folder.
