# [L4D & 2] Infected Slowdown Protect

### Introduction
- Prevent infected slowdown being overridden by other damage.
	- i.e. friendly-fire, spit, melee, claw.

<hr>

### Installation
1. Put the **l4d_infected_slowdown_protest.smx** to your _plugins_ folder.
