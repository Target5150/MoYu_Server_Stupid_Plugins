# [L4D & 2] Temp HP Prior

### Introduction
- Damage dealt to temporary health prior to permanent health.

<hr>

### Requirement
- **DHooks**

<hr>

### Installation
1. Put **plugins/l4d_temphp_prior.smx** to your _plugins_ folder.
2. Put **gamedata/l4d_temphp_prior.txt** to your _gamedata_ folder.
