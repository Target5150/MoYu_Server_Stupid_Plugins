# [L4D2 Only] Steady Boost

### Introduction
- Prevent forced sliding when landing at head of enemies.

<hr>

### Requirement
- **DHooks**

<hr>

### Installation
1. Put **plugins/l4d2_steady_boost.smx** to your _plugins_ folder.
2. Put **gamedata/l4d2_steady_boost.txt** to your _gamedata_ folder.
